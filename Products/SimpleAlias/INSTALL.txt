Installing SimpleAlias

Requirements:

* Plone 3.x
    
Install:
    * Copy the entire SimpleAlias folder to your zope Products folder and restart Zope
    * Use QuickInstaller or GenericSetup to install SimleAlias into your Plone site.
    
Go to your portal root in ZMI and enter the security tab. Go to 'SimpleAlias: Add Alias' permission
and configure it's roles.
